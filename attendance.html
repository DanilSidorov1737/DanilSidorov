Welcome to Attendance. This web-based program is designed
for teachers to make it easier to keep tracks of their students.
This program can keep track of when students enter and exit the school
go to the bathroom, or leave to another teachers class. This program
was designed by Danil Sidorov. Thank you for using this program today.



This web-based program is very simple to set up and use. To start of
have the students enter their information at the registration tab and
print or take a picture of the QR code. Then go to the scanner page
and have the students put the QR code up to the camera, and after it
scans it make sure they put the location, and if they are checking in or
out and then just click, scan to save the information, which you can access
on the History tab.
